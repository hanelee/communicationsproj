According to Washington Post, Gun sales in the US increased by 64% in 2020.
Thereby, based on FBI data, murder rate in the US increased by more than 25% in 2020.

Scroll

The first sentence implies that because of the alarming rise in crime,
people may be buying more guns to protect themselves.

The second sentence, on the other hand, implies that
because there are more guns, there is more crime.

Scroll

These two sentences were taken from two different articles in the Washington Post.

Because of the causation that the order of these sentences imply,
and also because of the authority we give news agencies and statistical figures,
it is fairly easy to write similarly believeable narratives, whether they are true or not.
